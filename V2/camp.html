Base Camp in Portugal

Welcome to our Base Camp in Portugal, where we are building a new eco-farm that embodies our commitment to sustainability and innovation.

~*~

Glamping Accommodation

To start, we offer comfortable glamping accommodations that allow you to experience the beauty of nature without sacrificing comfort. Stay in our luxurious tents and enjoy the serene landscapes while being close to the heart of our eco-friendly initiatives.

Vision: Growing and Expanding

Currently, we are planting new trees and expanding our land to create a thriving and sustainable farm. Our goal is to cultivate a diverse range of organic produce while enhancing the natural biodiversity of the region.

Future Plans

Looking ahead, we have exciting plans to establish a surf school, combining our love for nature with the thrill of surfing. This will provide visitors with an opportunity to connect with the ocean and embrace an active, eco-conscious lifestyle. Join us at Terra Nova - genesis and be a part of our journey towards a sustainable and adventurous future.
