Chapter 7. The Era of Light. The Tomb of Darkness


A hand turned the next page with great care. The flowing robe was rolled up on the arm to ensure it could not touch the pages. As the Wizard read, he emanated a deep reverence for the book. In the Era of Light, when the earthly universe breaks through the waters in which it was submerged, a new universe will be born. The sons and daughters of this new universe will perceive anew. They will dance in the gardens of knowledge and enjoy the fruits of other trees. They will unravel from the past mistakes of their race and live free from the old patterns that constrained their heart’s wisdom. They will discover their gold in the language of light, through which many separate existences live in the elegance of Unity. The Era of Light will emerge from many signs of the Creator’s creation. These signs will be encoded in a language unknown to all except the Creator and his servants: the Human Oracles. Many will attempt to interpret these signs, but they will fail to feel their significance. The signs will be drawn in the sand of human thoughts and interpreted by the imprisoned intellect; they will either be feared by wounded hearts or grasped by naive souls. However, the Human Oracles will overturn norms. They will shift values. They will destroy the frameworks of self-care that have infested the Earth. And so it is revealed that as the Oracle of Man becomes human, humans will become the Oracles of Light. This is the only sign that cannot be misinterpreted. It is the only signal from our Creator that a new universe is emerging from its cocoon and that the metamorphosis we have been waiting for stems from a shift visible to none but a select few. An elegant hand briefly touched the white beard and then wiped away a falling tear from the face of the humble master.
